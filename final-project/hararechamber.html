Harare Chamber of Commerce

Harare National Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit making membership-based organization that provides services designed to support its members in business development. As an independent organisation, the HCOC has represented the interests of its members for over 10 years especially through advocacy and provides a focus on business empowerment as the engine for economic growth. HCOC also aims to be a leader in business development in the local economy and a channel of communication between business and the various authorities in Harare.
