Why Solar?

Most of the modern-human needs are powered by electricity. Think of all the activities you did today. What portion of those activities interacted with electricity? Probably a majority of them. Now think of those who are not connected to electricity because their houses are not yet connected to grid electricity. How are they surviving? Ex Africa Energy provides the unconnected households with electricity to power their essential needs on a budget. In addition, the company supplies them with small solar gadgets that bring convenience to their lives.
